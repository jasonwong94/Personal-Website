The Spark Design Club designs and builds cool and interactive electrical and mechanical displays around campus. The club aims to provide students to enhance their soft skills by learning how to use power tools and solder circuitry. Projects are designed by the Spark exec team and are build with the help of Sparkers over a series of workshops.
