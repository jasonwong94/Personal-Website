Spark Design Club 2014-15 2015-16

Affiliated Club of the Year Award 20 Displays

The Spark Design Club designs and builds interactive electrical and mechanical displays around campus, promoting creativity around Skule. The club provides students opportunities to learn how to use power tools and solder circuitry through workshops where our displays are builts. Displays are designed by the Spark Exec Team.

During 2014-15, I was the webmaster and marketing executive; 2015-16, I was the co-president of the club. In both roles I assisted in planning the display, implementing and testing both hardware and software level using an Arduino.

University of Toronto Robotics Association (UTRA) 2014-15

UTRA is student led organization intended to make an name for robotics at UofT. Currently there are 4 divisions: Sumo, 3D Printing, Autonomous and Combat

The 3D Printing is one of the youngest divisions to be created. Being the co-lead, I taught participants the fundamentals of 3D printing. From modelling in Solidworks to operating a RepRap 3D printer. Weekly workshops were held to teach participants various topics such as maintaince and basic operation.
